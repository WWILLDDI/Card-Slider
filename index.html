Dr. Pepper Original

Dr Pepper was created in the 1880s by pharmacist Charles Alderton in Waco, Texas, and first served around 1885. Dr Pepper was first nationally marketed in the United States in 1904. It is now also sold in Europe, Asia, North and South America.

Canada Dry

Canada Dry was founded in 1904 and owned since 2008 by the American company Keurig Dr Pepper. With the same taste as Diet, it combines the classic ginger taste of Canada Dry Zero Sugar Ginger Ale with a splash of lemonade made with real lemon juice.

La Croix

LaCroix is the bright and trendy drink we can't miss in the sparkling water section of the grocery store. It has numerous flavors to try, each an excellent alternative to soda. So, a can of LaCroix will do you some good if you're on a strict diet.

Watermelon Soda

Enjoy the 'nutrition & taste' of everyone's favourite fruit - the humble watermelon - in this brand new soda can all the way from Korea. This tasty beverage is packed full of an intense but refreshing flavour that tastes exactly like a watermelon should.

Coca Cola Zero

Coca-Cola Zero was Coca-Cola's largest product launch in 22 years. The global campaign was developed by creative agency Crispin Porter + Bogusky.It was marketed as having a taste that is indistinguishable from standard Coca-Cola, as opposed to Diet Coke, which has a different flavor profile.

Montain Dew

Mountain Dew was first created in the 1940s by two Tennessee brothers. The soda has a unique flavor that is both sweet and tart, due to the combination of citrusy and caffeinated flavors. Mountain Dew is also less carbonated than other sodas, which makes it more refreshing.

Sidral Mundet

Sidral Mundet is Mexico's original apple soda and part of Mexico's heritage since 1902, is a natural, healthy family favorite made with real apple juice and believed to have medical benefits due to its pasteurization process.

Crush Cream

Crush Cream Soda, Released in Canada and loved by every Canadian since 1916. Crush Cream Soda delivers a sweet, creamy goodness to your taste buds each time! The perfect size to bring to your next party or get together.

7 Up

7 Up is first introduced in 1929 in the United States. It is the main drink in the 7 Up line and is commonly referred to as the "original" flavor in the world that is being sold in stores, restaurants, and vending machines.
